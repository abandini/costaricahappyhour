The Drinks of Costa Rica

From the national spirit to craft breweries, Costa Rica's happy hour offers unique flavors you won't find anywhere else.

Guaro & Cacique

The national spirit - a clear sugarcane liquor similar to vodka but naturally sweeter. Try it in a Guaro Sour or the spicy Chiliguaro shot.

Imperial & Pilsen

Imperial (since 1924) with its black eagle logo and Pilsen (since 1888) dominate the beer scene - light, refreshing, perfect for the tropics.

Craft Beer Revolution

Costa Rica Craft Brewing, Monteverde Brewing Co, and San Roque Brewery lead a growing scene using local fruits like mango and coconut.

Signature Cocktails

Try the Chiliguaro (guaro + hot sauce + lime), Michelada (beer cocktail), or a Coco Loco served in a fresh coconut.
